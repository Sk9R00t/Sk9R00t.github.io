My Dearest Preety,

Today, as we celebrate your birthday, I want to take a moment to truly tell you what you mean to me. You are, without a doubt, my most beautiful human being.

You are so much more than a lover; you are the light of my life the warm, steady glow that guides me through everything. You are my constant inspiration, pushing me to be better, kinder, and happier simply by being you.

Your smile has a magical way of brightening even the darkest day, and your infectious laugh is my favorite sound in the entire world. When I feel overwhelmed, it is your presence and your steady love that gives me the strength to keep going. You are my home, my confidante, and my greatest adventure.

Thank you for being born, for choosing me, and for filling my life with a depth of love I never knew was possible. I cherish every moment we share.

Happy Birthday, my darling. May your day be as magnificent as the soul you possess.

With all my love,
Abid
